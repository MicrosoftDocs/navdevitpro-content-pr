# AL Simple Statements
AL simple statements are single-line statements that are executed sequentially and do not alter the flow of execution of code. The following are the types of simple statements in C/AL:  
  
-   [AL Assignment Statements](devenv-al-assignment-statements.md)  
  
-   [AL Function Statements](devenv-al-method-statements.md)  
  
-   [AL ASSERTERROR Statements](devenv-al-asserterror-statements.md)  
  
-   [AL WITH Statements](devenv-al-with-statements.md)
